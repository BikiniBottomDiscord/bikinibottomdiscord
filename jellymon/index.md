### Intro
Jellymon is a Pokemon-style game where you hunt, catch, fight, and trade wild Jellymons. Jellymons come in different shapes and styles, with abilities and features similar to their Jellyfish Fields counterparts. The name of the game? Catch and collect all Jellymons! While doing so, participate in battles and ranked battles to increase your prestige and become a Club Master!
